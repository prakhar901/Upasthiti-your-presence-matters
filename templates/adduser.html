Please read the following instructions carefully before capturing images for the real-time face attendance system:

  1. Make sure the room is well-lit.
  2. Sit in front of a neutral background.
  3. Remove any accessories that cover your face.
  4. Look straight at the camera.
  5. Take multiple pictures from different angles and positions.
  6. Take pictures with and without glasses.
  7. Follow any additional instructions provided by your instructor.
